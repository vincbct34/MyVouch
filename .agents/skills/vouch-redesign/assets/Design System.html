01 — The idea

A reference you can stand behind

Vouch sits between a testimonial wall and a background check. Every endorsement is tied to a real working relationship, passes through your review, and carries a verified mark when it's published. The brand has to feel two things at once: warm enough to invite honesty, and credible enough to be believed.

Principle 01

Credible, not corporate

Editorial serif for the human voice of a quote; a precise grotesk for the system around it. Trust is shown, not shouted.

Principle 02

Verification is the hero

The check-in-a-bubble runs through the whole product. Green always means a real person, really verified.

Principle 03

You stay in control

Nothing goes public without your approval. The admin surfaces make moderation calm, clear and quick.

02 — Logo

The mark

A speech bubble and a checkmark, fused. The quote is the endorsement; the check is the verification. One shape carries the entire promise of the product.
